About OdLeaighCreative

My name is Shannon O'Donnell and I am OdLeaighCreative. I am a single mother, full-time Content Management, SharePoint, and Technology specialist for the Human Services group at Florida Blue. I suffer from bipolar which is paired with severe anxiety. Because of this, I do not know how to interact with others. I try not to let it run my life, but to some degree it always will.

History

I started my career as a bookkeeper. However, since I was raised by a helpdesk technician, I had a natural talent for all things technology and consistently found myself handling technology tasks. Depending upon the company, these tasks ranged from fixing the printer to designing and troubleshooting code.

I knew I liked solving problems, so every time I was presented with something I didn't know, I taught myself how to handle it. This included javascript, html, css, visual basic, xml, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and more.

In one job, I was asked to handle the ordering for all marketing material for the company, which evolved into designing it as well. This is when I discovered my passion for code and design. I began learning everything I could, but when you learn on the job, you only learn the scenarios you come across. Because of this, I was stuck in the boat of not knowing what I didn't know.

I have been in my current position for almost 4 years, and loving it, so I am now working towards a degree in Information Technology with a focus on Web design and development.
